PET monofilament is primarily used for the purposes of textile market.

The main fields of application are the production of curtain tapes, conveyor belts, tensile and binding belts, zips for clothing industry and filters for the paper industry.

Due to the nature of the raw material (its high melting point) and the formulation procedure it is subjected to, this type of monofilament presents great resistance against high temperatures and abrasion. This characteristic is easily recognised by the amount of resistance this monofilament presents against deformability and by its good reaction to chemical agents.

The PET monofilament line comes in its natural colour(white). It is available in the following dimensions: 0,10-0,29mm.
