The branch of Techmonfil in Athens(at 75 Kefallinias Str., Moschato), has been the place of production of monofilament lines since 1972.

Part of the initial equipment came under the today’s owners ownership in 1988, when a change of proprietorship of the 1972-founded company occurred. That point of time initiated the modernization of the company and the struggling effort for the production of the most up-to-date monofilament products. In the following 15 years of its function, the “KAPSILIS-TRIANTIS” company which was later on renamed as “TECHMONFIL-K.TRIANTIS & Co general partnership” enriched its range of products and established itself as the leading and most reliable monofilament lines productor in Greece. At the same period the amount of our exports was continuously increasing.

The consistent need to increase and modernize our production led to the construction of a new, fully equipped industrial building of 2500 square meters in Palio Chorio, Ag. Theodoroi, Korinthos, where the head offices were transferred in 2006. Due to the new funds that we have invested in the equipment that we use for our line of products, the quality assurance system (according to the EN ISO 9001:2000 system) and the training of our human resources, we can take pride in being one of the most modernized contemporary monofilament producing companies worldwide.

We offer:

  • One of the most advantageous quality-price ratios in the world market.
  • The credibility that the values referred on the tags of our products(meters, max load tenacity, etc.) respond to reality.
  • The assurance that our products, each and everyone of which always bears a unique production code on its tag, have been thoroughly controlled before being released in the market.
